Sustainable Patrol Routing in Chicago

芝加哥可持续巡逻路线规划

Team 6 | DDA3003 Visual Analytics, Spring 2026

第六小组 | DDA3003 可视分析，2026年春季

Abstract

摘要

This project focuses on sustainable patrol routing in Chicago to achieve SDG16 (Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions) and SDG13 (Climate Action). We propose a Dynamic Spatio-Temporal Patrol Optimization (DSTPO) framework integrating STSGCN and ConvLSTM, aiming to maximize crime hot-spot coverage, minimize carbon emissions, and reduce response time while mitigating enforcement bias.

本项目聚焦芝加哥可持续巡逻路线规划，旨在实现SDG16（和平、正义与强大机构）与SDG13（气候行动）目标。我们提出融合STSGCN和ConvLSTM的动态时空巡逻优化（DSTPO）框架，力求最大化犯罪热点覆盖、最小化碳排放、缩短响应时间，同时缓解执法偏差。

Workflow

工作流

  • Define objectives and review relevant literature
  • Collect and preprocess Chicago crime/traffic/mobility data
  • Implement ML/DL models (STSGCN + SEPP) for spatio-temporal prediction
  • Train and fine-tune models on GPU clusters
  • Evaluate model performance with baselines and metrics
  • Visualize results for intuitive interpretation
  • 明确研究目标并梳理相关文献
  • 收集并预处理芝加哥犯罪/交通/人口流动数据
  • 实现机器学习/深度学习模型（STSGCN + SEPP）进行时空预测
  • 在GPU集群上训练并微调模型
  • 结合基线模型与评估指标验证模型性能
  • 可视化结果以实现直观解读

Innovative & Impact

创新与影响

Innovation:

创新点：

  • Multi-source data fusion (crime + traffic + mobility + energy consumption)
  • Spatio-temporal modeling (STSGCN + SEPP) for dynamic risk prediction
  • Multi-objective optimization (SDG16 + SDG13: coverage, emission, response time)
